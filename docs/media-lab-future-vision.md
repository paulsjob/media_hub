# Media Lab Future Vision

The full Media Lab command center is deferred.

Future product concepts may include story intake, approval queues, asset libraries, distribution planning, performance reporting, publishing workflows, and AI-assisted production. Those areas are intentionally outside the current active product surface.

## Deferred Areas

- Dashboard and command-center overview
- Story records and intake queues
- Approval workflow
- Library, archive, and metadata management
- Distribution planner and publishing handoff
- Performance reporting
- AI assist and content calendar

## Current Priority

Media Lab Lite should remain fast, obvious, and reliable before expanding. The Quote Card workflow should keep preview generation, MoDeck rendering, status polling, thumbnails, and downloads working across connected sizes.
